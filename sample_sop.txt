STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
Title: Customer Complaint Handling Process
Department: Customer Service
Version: 2.1

1. PURPOSE
This SOP establishes a standardized process for receiving, documenting,
investigating, and resolving customer complaints to ensure consistent
service quality and customer satisfaction.

2. SCOPE
Applies to all customer service representatives, team leads, and managers
handling complaints via phone, email, or in-person.

3. RESPONSIBILITIES
- Customer Service Rep: First contact, logs complaint in CRM
- Team Lead: Reviews unresolved complaints after 24 hours
- Manager: Handles escalations and approves refunds above $500

4. PROCEDURE

Step 1: Receive and Acknowledge
Greet professionally. Listen without interrupting. Acknowledge with empathy.

Step 2: Log the Complaint
Enter into CRM within 5 minutes: customer name, contact info, date/time,
nature of issue, product involved, severity (Low/Medium/High).

Step 3: Investigate
Review account history. Consult billing/logistics/tech if needed.
Timeframes: High = 4 hours, Medium = 24 hours, Low = 48 hours.

Step 4: Resolve
Offer replacement, refund, or credit. CSR approves up to $500.
Above $500 requires manager sign-off. Confirm resolution with customer.

Step 5: Document Resolution
Update CRM. Mark ticket Resolved only after customer confirms satisfaction.

Step 6: Follow Up
Call or email within 48 hours to confirm continued satisfaction. Log in CRM.

5. ESCALATION
Unresolved complaints past deadline escalate to Team Lead immediately.
Repeat complaints within 30 days go to manager.

6. COMPLIANCE
Records retained for 3 years minimum. Data handled per Privacy Policy.
